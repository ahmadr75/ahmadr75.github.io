Ahmad Rezaei is a young researcher at RSS lab., where he works on Hardware Design and Machine Learning.
He received his bachelor in Electrical Engineering- Minor Electronics at SBUK university, in 2019. His
bachelor dissertation was on "Developing Dynamic Bayesian networks for SET analysis in digital circuits"
under the supervision of Prof. Ali Mahani.

Commencing his programming life with Q-basic and Visual Basic languages at age 12, He was a student 
in National Organization for Development of Exceptional Talents (NODET Special School). He was further
taught C/C++ languages before graduating from school; nowadays, he thinks of programming languages more 
of a tool to serve the challenges.
